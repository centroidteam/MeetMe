Welcome to Meet Me.

Meet Me is a quick, easy-to-use way to find the best places to meet with your friends! Starting as a project at a local Hackathon, Meet Me hit us as a legitimate app idea when it took first place at GunnHacks as the location-managing application, Centroid. Since then, Meet Me has changed, both in name and in shape. In the last three months, the developers have updated the user interface and added features to make finding the best meeting location with your crew as easy as a few taps!
